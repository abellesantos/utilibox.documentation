# Contract Transfers

Managing contract transfers can be complex, with roll-ins, roll-outs, and constant record updates to track. The Contract Transfers page simplifies this process by giving you tools to add, update, or delete transfers, filter records for quick access, and seamlessly import or export dataâ€”ensuring every contract move is clear and under control.

### Adding a Contract Transfer

1. From the Finance tab, click on Contract Transfers.
2. On the upper right hand side of the page, click on the +Add New button.
3. Once the Transfer Request Details window opens, fill in the necessary details and click Next.
4. Choose if the request was initiated with the retailer by ticking the box, but this is optional. Click Next.
5. From the Reference Information window, select the linked contract (another window will open where user can choose the available contracts in UB) and input the case id related to the request. Click Next.
6. Review all the information and click Save to submit.

### Editing a Contract Transfer

1. From the Finance tab, click on Contract Transfers.
2. Select the contract transfer and click on the edit button at the far right of the row.
3. Once the Transfer Request Details window opens, update the fields as necessary and click Next.
4. From the Transfer Status Details window and Reference information, update the fields as necessary and click Next.
5. Review all the information and click Save to submit.

### Deleting a Contract Transfer

1. From the Finance tab, click on Contract Transfers.
2. Select the contract transfer and click on the delete button at the far right of the row.
3. Confirm deletion and click Ok.
4. Status of deletion will appear at the top of the page.
