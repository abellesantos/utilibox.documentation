# Decarbonisation Planner

This feature helps organisations map out how to reduce their emissions and achieve Net Zero through data, modelling, and strategic planning.

This is where users can update the abatement applicable to their net zero workflow by adding, editing or deleting records.

### Updating Abatement

1. Click on Update Abatement on the upper right corner of the screen.
2. A window will open where you can choose to edit, delete existing abatements or add a new one.
3. From the Unmodified Abatement tabs, user can click on the edit/delete/add button.
   1. Edit - click on the pencil icon and from the pop-up windows, update the fields as necessary and click save.
   2. Add - click on the +Add Abatement on the upper right corner of the screen. From the pop-up windows, fill in the fields as required. Click Save
   3. Delete - click on the trash bin button and it will automatically be removed from the list
4. Once saved, the record will be shown in the appropriate tab (Edited, Deleted, Added).
5. When refreshing a specific scenario after an update, simply click on the “Refresh Scenario” button on the upper right corner of the screen.
